by Sébastien Abilla (Seb46)

#### Play Online:

[itch.io](https://seb46.itch.io/the-assembly-of-decomposers)

#### Description:

* Made for Velox Turbo 3. Theme: The world is ending but I still have a job.
* A 5 min interactive fiction about those who will still have a job after the end of our world.
